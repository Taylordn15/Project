Overview

The Uchiha clan is one of the many clans within the village hidden in the leaves, also known as Konaha. The Uhciha are natrually gifted ninja that specialize in fire release. They are also incharge of the village's police force. The Uchiha are feared throughout the ninja world mainly because of their visual prowess.

Clan Symbol

Uchiha in another way of pronouncing "uchiha," a paper fan, which is incidentally the Uchiha clan symbol. Uchiwa can be used to fan flames, making the flame hotter — referring to the fact that the Uchiha is a clan of fire-jutsu users.

Fire Release

Fire Release (火遁, Katon) is one of the basic elemental nature transformations. It is performed by moulding superheated chakra inside the stomach before releasing it via the lungs and mouth. There are also variants to this in the form of some mediums such as the use of gun powder, explosive tags and chakra flow into a weapon.
